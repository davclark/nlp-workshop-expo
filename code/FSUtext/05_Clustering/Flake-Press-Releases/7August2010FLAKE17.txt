REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

LENGTH: 93 words


ESA, Ariz., Aug. 6 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the omnibus appropriations bill
for fiscal year 2010.

This week's egregious earmark: $500,000 for improvements to Elvis Presley
Boulevard in Tennessee.
