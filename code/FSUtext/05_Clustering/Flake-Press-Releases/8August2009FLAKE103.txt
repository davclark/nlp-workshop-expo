REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

LENGTH: 87  words


ESA, Ariz., Aug. 6 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the omnibus spending bill for
fiscal year 2009.

This week's egregious earmark: $1,037,000 for potato research in Washington,
Maine, and Idaho.

"Earmarks are like potato chips - you can't have just one," said Flake.For more
