REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 80  words

DATELINE: MESA, Ariz.


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the Transportation-HUD
Appropriations bill for fiscal year 2008.

This week's egregious earmark: $250,000 for construction of a one million gallon
water storage tower in Ada, Oklahoma.

"Even earmarks to hold water don't hold water," said Flake.
