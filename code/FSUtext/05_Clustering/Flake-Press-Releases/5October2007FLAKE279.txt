REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 84  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the Transportation-Treasury-HUD
Appropriations bill for fiscal year 2006.

This week's egregious earmark: $300,000 to the University of New England for
construction and equipment at the George and Barbara Bush Cultural Center.

"Read my lips: no new earmarks," said Flake.
