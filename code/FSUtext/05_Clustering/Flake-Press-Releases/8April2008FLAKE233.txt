REP. FLAKE GETS HIGHEST SCORE IN CONGRESS FROM NATIONAL TAXPAYERS UNION

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 117  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today received the highest score from the National Taxpayers Union on their
annual scorecard rating of Members of Congress on fiscal policy beneficial to
taxpayers.

The National Taxpayers Union compiles the score by reviewing every congressional
vote that affects tax, debt, spending, and regulatory issues.

For the fifth year in a row, Congressman Flake received the highest score in the
House of Representatives.

More information on the National Taxpayers Union's scorecard can be found at
http://www.ntu.org.
