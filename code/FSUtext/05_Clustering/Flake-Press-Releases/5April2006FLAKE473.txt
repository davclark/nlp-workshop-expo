REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 75  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Republican Rep. Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District, today
highlighted a pork project contained in the Agriculture appropriations bill for
fiscal year 2006.

This week's egregious earmark: $229,000 for dairy education in Iowa.

"This may sound cheesy but somebody needs to tell Congress, 'that's nacho
money,'" said Flake.
