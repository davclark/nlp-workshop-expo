REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

LENGTH: 101  words


WASHINGTON, March 6 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the omnibus spending bill for
fiscal year 2009, which the House of Representatives passed last week. This
week's egregious earmark: $550,000 for Chesapeake Bay blue crab research in
Annapolis, Maryland. "Taxpayers are already feeling blue, but now they'll really
be feeling the pinch," said Flake.For more information please contact: Sarabjit
Jagirdar, Email:- htsyndication@hindustantimes.com
