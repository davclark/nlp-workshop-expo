REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF WEEK

LENGTH: 89  words


WASHINGTON, Feb. 5 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted an earmark contained in the omnibus appropriations bill for
fiscal year 2010.

This week's egregious earmark: $500,000 for the Chesapeake Bay Interpretive Buoy
System.

"Taxpayers' confidence in Congress sure isn't buoyed by the wave of earmarks,"
