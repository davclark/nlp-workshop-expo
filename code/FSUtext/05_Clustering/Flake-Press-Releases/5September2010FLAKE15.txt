REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF WEEK

LENGTH: 97  words


ESA, Ariz., Sept. 3 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the Energy and Water
appropriations bill for fiscal year 2010.

This week's egregious earmark: $100,000 to support flood control infrastructure
improvements in El Paso County, Texas.
