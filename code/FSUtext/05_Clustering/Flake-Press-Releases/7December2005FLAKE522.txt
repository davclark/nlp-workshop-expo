REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 83  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Arizona Rep. Jeff Flake, who represents the state's Sixth District, today
highlighted a pork project contained in the Transportation-Treasury
appropriations bill for fiscal year 2006.

This week's egregious earmark: $250,000 to the City of Utica, New York for the
replacement of windows at the Utica Public Library.

"It panes me that Congress would fund an earmark like this," said Flake.
